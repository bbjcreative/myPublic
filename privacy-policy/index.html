myPublic

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 June 2026

myPublic ("we", "our", or "the app") is a free Malaysian public transit application developed by WZB Group. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and your rights under Malaysia's Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA).

1. Data We Collect

Location (GPS)

  • Used to show transit stops near you and to trigger get-off haptic alerts during navigation.
  • Collected only while the app is in use (foreground), or in the background during an active navigation session.
  • Not stored on our servers. Processed locally on your device or passed anonymously to our backend to query nearby stops.

Push Notification Token (FCM)

  • An opaque device token provided by Google Firebase Cloud Messaging.
  • Stored in our database only if you opt in to disruption alerts.
  • Used exclusively to deliver service disruption notifications for routes you subscribe to.
  • Deleted from our database when you disable notifications in Settings.

JPJ Data (vehicle plate & IC number)

  • Entered voluntarily in the JPJ Checker feature.
  • Never stored by myPublic. The data is transmitted directly to the Malaysian Government's mygdx.gov.my API and the response is shown to you. We do not log or retain this information.

App Preferences

  • Language preference (English / Bahasa Malaysia) and saved routes are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage.
  • This data never leaves your device.

Crash & Diagnostic Data

  • If the app crashes, Firebase Crashlytics automatically sends a report to us containing the error, device model, OS version, and app version.
  • No personally identifiable information is included in crash reports.

Analytics

  • Firebase Analytics collects anonymous usage data: which screens you visit, session duration, and device type.
  • This data is aggregated and cannot identify you individually.
  • On iOS, you will be asked for permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt before analytics are activated.

2. Advertising

myPublic is free and supported by ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect your device's advertising ID and IP address to serve relevant ads. Ads are never shown during active navigation (NavigationScreen) as a safety measure. To opt out of personalised ads, you can reset or limit your device's advertising ID in your phone's Settings.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google Maps SDK — powers the in-app map. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob — ad serving. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase (Crashlytics, Analytics, Remote Config) — app health and configuration. Subject to Firebase's Privacy Policy.
  • AWS (Lambda, DynamoDB, CloudFront) — our backend infrastructure. Data is processed in the ap-southeast-5 (Kuala Lumpur) AWS region.
  • data.gov.my — Malaysian Government open data API for GTFS transit schedules.
  • mygdx.gov.my — Malaysian Government JPJ API. Only queried when you use the JPJ Checker feature.

4. Data Retention

  • Push notification tokens: retained until you disable notifications in the app, or until the token expires (typically 60 days of inactivity).
  • Crash reports: retained in Firebase for 90 days.
  • Analytics data: aggregated data retained per Google's standard retention schedule.
  • All other user data (preferences, saved routes): stored only on your device.

5. Your Rights Under PDPA 2010

Under Malaysia's Personal Data Protection Act 2010, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Withdrawal of consent — opt out of push notifications at any time via the app Settings or your device notification settings.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your push notification token from our servers.

To exercise these rights, contact us at jayp@wzbgroup.my. We will respond within 21 days.

6. Children's Privacy

myPublic is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
